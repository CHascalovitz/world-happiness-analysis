Data Source

The dataset for the World Happiness Report was downloaded from Kaggle and the dataset for the Gini Coefficient from The World Bank.

The WHS is based on surveys conducted by Gallup World Poll. These surveys ask people from various countries to rate their subjective well-being and happiness based on different factors such as income, social support, freedom to make life choices, generosity, perceptions of corruption, and healthy life expectancy. The data is then compiled and analyzed to produce the World Happiness Report, which ranks countries based on their happiness scores and provides insights into factors influencing happiness levels globally.

The database includes data from 156 countries from 2019.

There are eight datasets:

  • World Happiness Score
  • GDP per Capita
  • Social Support
  • Healthy Life Expectancy
  • Freedom to Make Life Choices
  • Generosity
  • Perceptions of Corruption
  • Gini Coefficient
